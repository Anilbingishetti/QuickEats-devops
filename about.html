About QuickEats

Vision

QuickEats envisions becoming the most trusted and loved food delivery brand, bringing people closer to their favorite meals with speed, reliability, and care. Our goal is to revolutionize the dining experience by combining technology, efficiency, and a passion for great food.

Mission

To provide a seamless and delightful food delivery experience by connecting customers to top restaurants and local chefs, ensuring fresh, fast, and affordable meals. We strive to empower small food businesses and promote sustainability in every delivery we make.

Leadership

Founder & CEO - Mr. Arjun Rao

With a passion for technology and good food, Arjun founded QuickEats in 2020 with the vision to make high-quality meals accessible to everyone, anytime. His leadership focuses on innovation, customer satisfaction, and operational excellence.

Head of Operations - Ms. Priya Sharma

Priya oversees daily logistics and ensures timely deliveries across all cities. She has over a decade of experience in the food-tech industry and is committed to maintaining QuickEats’ promise of fast and reliable service.
